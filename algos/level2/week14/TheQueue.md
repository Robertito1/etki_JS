### THE QUEUE 

#### About the queue: 
- a data structure based on FIFO Principle (**F**irst **I**n **F**irst **O**ut)
- **enqueuing:** adding a new record to the beginning of the Queue (first index)
- **dequeuing:** removing the oldest record from the Queue (last index)
