### INTEGER REVERSAL

#### Directions
  Given a string, return an integer that is the reverse ordering of numbers.


#### Examples
  reverseInt(15) === 51
  reverseInt(981) === 189
  reverseInt(500) === 5
  reverseInt(-15) === -51
  reverseInt(-90) === -9
